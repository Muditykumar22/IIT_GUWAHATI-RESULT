University Norms & Conditions

  1. Students must maintain a minimum attendance of 75% in all courses to be eligible for examinations.
  2. All fees once paid are non-refundable and non-transferable under any circumstances.
  3. The institute reserves the right to modify academic rules, regulations, and fee structures as deemed necessary.
  4. Students must maintain discipline and decorum within the campus premises at all times.
  5. Academic integrity is mandatory; any form of plagiarism or cheating will result in strict disciplinary action.
  6. Use of official ID card is compulsory for entry into campus and access to various facilities.
  7. Ragging in any form is strictly prohibited and is a punishable offense under the law.
  8. Students must follow all examination rules and regulations; violation will lead to cancellation of results.
  9. Regular submission of assignments and project work is mandatory for course completion.
  10. Students must inform the administration about any change in personal details within 15 days.
  11. Library resources must be used responsibly; damage or loss of books will incur penalties.
  12. Use of mobile phones and electronic devices during lectures and examinations is prohibited.
  13. Students must participate in all mandatory workshops, seminars, and industrial visits as scheduled.
  14. Hostel rules and regulations must be strictly adhered to by all resident students.
  15. Any form of substance abuse or misconduct will result in immediate expulsion from the institute.
  16. Students are required to maintain a minimum CGPA of 6.0 to remain in good academic standing.
  17. All grievances must be submitted through proper channels and official complaint procedures.
  18. Students must complete all course requirements within the stipulated time frame of the program.
  19. Participation in co-curricular and extra-curricular activities is encouraged but must not affect academic performance.
  20. The institute's decision on all academic and disciplinary matters shall be final and binding.
